Cabot Cecil

I am currently a Business Intelligence QA Engineer at Churchill Downs. I graduated with my bachelors from Centre College in economics and finance, and received my masters in business analytics from the University of Louisville. Both my professional and personal goals revolve around building transferable skills in order to make myself more efficacious. In my professional life, that means learning to leverage technology and data to better assist my general problem solving abilities, whether the problem is a financial question, or a strategy to stop preventable illness. My professional experience in business analytics has given me opportunities to hone my skills in creating data pipelines, visualizations, models, forecasts and reports using Python, SQL, Azure, Power BI and numerous other tools. In my personal life, I build transferable skills through reading philosophy, building machine learning models and playing board games, all of which involve reducing complex systems into their essential applications, then recombining to produce something novel and useful. My ultimate goal is to accumulate the skills necessary to understand, explain and predict the facets of the society that should be improved, mainly sustainability and social equality.
